Blurb

Feyre is a 19-year-old huntress who kills a wolf in the woods and is dragged into the faerie realm of Prythian as punishmentonly to discover her captors world is not what it seems. A seductive Beauty-and-the-Beast retelling packed with dangerous magic, slow-burn romance, and court intrigue.

Why I picked it

Wanted to see why the ACOTAR series dominates BookTok and test the hype around Sarah J. Maass fae romances.

First impressions

The opening feels YA-grim, but the world-building ramps quickly once Feyre crosses the wall. Curious to track the shift from survival thriller to high-court politics.
